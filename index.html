Kigali Commercial Complex

Kigali, Rwanda
Completed: June 2022
25,000 blocks

This 8-story mixed-use development in the heart of Kigali required a complex materials solution. We provided complete end-to-end services including:

  • Custom block manufacturing to meet architectural specifications
  • Just-in-time delivery coordination with multiple contractors
  • Quality control testing throughout the project
  • Technical support for specialized applications

The project was completed on time and within budget, with our materials meeting all structural requirements for this high-rise development.

Rural School Project

Northern Province, Rwanda
Completed: March 2023
8,000 blocks

For this community school project in a remote area, we established a temporary production facility on-site to:

  • Reduce transportation costs and environmental impact
  • Provide employment opportunities for local residents
  • Ensure fresh materials were always available
  • Adapt production to the specific needs of the project

Our mobile production unit operated for 3 months, manufacturing all blocks needed for the 12-classroom school while training local workers in block production techniques.

Luxury Residential Estate

Kigali Heights, Rwanda
Completed: December 2021
42,000 blocks

This high-end residential development required specialized materials to meet the architect's exacting standards. Our solutions included:

  • Custom block sizes and colors to match design specifications
  • Precision manufacturing for consistent quality
  • Strict quality control for every batch
  • Coordinated delivery schedule with multiple contractors

The project included 15 luxury villas, each with unique architectural features that required customized block solutions. Our team worked closely with the architects to ensure all materials met their exact requirements.

Our Story

Building Rwanda's Future

Combining manufacturing excellence with logistics innovation

Founded in 2010, BTN Group has grown from a small concrete block manufacturer to Rwanda's leading integrated construction solutions provider. Our unique combination of manufacturing capabilities and logistics expertise allows us to deliver unparalleled value to projects of all scales.

What sets us apart is our ability to establish temporary production facilities at large project sites, reducing transportation costs and ensuring fresh materials are always available.
